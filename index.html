FAQ
Questions.
Do I need training? +
You don't need to be an EMT, but a basic first aid and CPR class is strongly recommended. You're also expected to review all educational materials — video tutorials for each item, articles, and field guide — included with the kit.
Can you customize it? +
While I recommend the Superhero tier for every activity (even driving your car), I can work with you to create the perfect kit for your specific needs. Reach out directly.
How many people can one kit help? +
I strongly recommend one kit per person. Multiple kits reduce risk of loss, increase speed of care when your group is spread out, and can be combined for severe injuries — some incidents require multiple tourniquets to stop a bleed.
How does it attach? +
Designed to mount to a climbing harness, running vest, or backpack via a combination of carabiners, cords, and webbing.
Can I see the full contents list? +
Yes — text or email directly to get the complete contents list. (651) 395-1906 or tbengels@gmail.com.
What about comfort items like tweezers or SAM splints? +
The Superhero kit focuses on life-saving interventions while staying ultralight. I'm happy to include comfort and maintenance items (tweezers, ointments, SAM splints, blister treatment, irrigation tools, etc.) for an additional $50.
